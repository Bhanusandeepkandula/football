Match Center

Privacy Policy

Effective 10 July 2026 · Last updated 10 July 2026

Match Center is a football scores and news app. It is built to work with as little of your data as possible: almost everything stays on your device, we have no user accounts, and we do not track you or sell anything about you.

Who we are

Match Center (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is developed by Kandula Bhanu Sandeep. You can reach us about privacy at sandeepkandula08@gmail.com.

The short version

  • No account, no sign-up. You use the app anonymously.
  • Your preferences (followed teams, alert settings, theme) are stored only on your device. We can’t see them.
  • We don’t run advertising SDKs, and we don’t track you across other apps or websites.
  • The one time data leaves your device is to deliver match notifications you turned on — and only the minimum needed to do that.

Information stored on your device

The following is saved locally on your phone (using the operating system’s standard app storage) and is never transmitted to us:

  • Teams, leagues and matches you follow or favourite.
  • Your notification and Live Activity preferences.
  • Your chosen theme and display settings.

Deleting the app removes all of this from your device.

Information we receive (only if you enable notifications)

If you turn on match alerts or a Live Activity for a game, your device gives us the technical identifiers Apple requires to deliver those updates while the app is closed. We send these to our notification service:

Push token(s)An anonymous, device-specific token issued by Apple Push Notification service (APNs), plus the Live Activity token for a game you’re watching. These let Apple route a notification to your device. They are not your name, email or phone number and cannot identify you personally.
Matches you subscribed toThe IDs of the games you asked to be alerted about, so we know which score updates to send you.
Alert preferencese.g. goals-only vs. all events, so we send only what you asked for.

We use this solely to send the notifications you requested (goals, cards, kickoff, half-time, full-time and Live Activity score updates). We do not use it for advertising or profiling. When you turn a match’s alerts off, unsubscribe, or the token becomes invalid, the corresponding record is deleted from our server. We do not keep push tokens longer than needed to deliver alerts.

If you never enable notifications, no data about you leaves your device at all.

Content we fetch from third parties

To show scores, tables, stats, news and media, the app requests publicly available content from third-party sources. These requests deliver content to your device; we do not send your personal information to these providers. As with any internet request, the provider may receive your device’s IP address and standard network information. Sources include:

  • Sports data and statistics providers (for scores, fixtures, standings, lineups and player data).
  • News publishers (headlines and article links) and social platforms (public football posts and clips).
  • Video and image hosts (for highlights and team/player imagery).

Each provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle requests. Opening an external link or video may take you to that provider, where their terms and privacy practices apply.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.
  • We do not use advertising networks or ad identifiers.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites (no cross-app tracking).
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children (see below).

Analytics

The current version does not include third-party analytics or crash-tracking SDKs. If a future version adds privacy-respecting, aggregated diagnostics to improve reliability, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy label before that version ships, and such data will not be used to identify you.

Children’s privacy

Match Center is a general-audience app and is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Data security & retention

On-device data is protected by your device’s standard app sandboxing and encryption. The limited notification data described above is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/APNs) and retained only while you have alerts enabled for a match; invalid or unsubscribed tokens are removed automatically.

Your choices & rights

  • Notifications: turn match alerts and Live Activities on or off at any time in the app or in iOS Settings. Turning them off deletes the related server record.
  • Local data: delete the app to erase everything stored on your device.
  • Access/deletion: because we hold no account and no directly identifying data, there is little personal information to access — but you may contact us with any privacy request. Depending on where you live (e.g. the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access or delete personal data; we will honour applicable requests.

International users

Our notification service may process the limited data above on servers located outside your country. We only process what is necessary to deliver the alerts you requested.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new “Last updated” date, and where required, surfaced in the app.
